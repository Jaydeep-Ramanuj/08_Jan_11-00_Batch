Engine

As shown in the referenced figure, an automotive engine is a positive displacement internal combustion engine having an intake, compression, combustion and exhaust stroke. A gas turbine engine is a dynamic internal combustion engine.

Vehicle exhaust

Your vehicle's exhaust system is made up of a complex system of pipes and devices that serve four essential functions: collect gases from the engine, remove harmful toxins from those gases, reduce engine noise and discharge the gases away from the car's occupants
